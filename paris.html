Paris - The city of love..

There’s a lot to see in Paris. It’s hard to get started, so it’s best to prepare an itinerary with a map ahead. Some of the essential places would be:
- Louvre Museum, Musée d'Orsay and Centre George Pompidou - Eiffel Tower - Notre Dame - Arc de Triomphe and Champs Elysées - Sacred Heart and Montmartre - Pantheon - Saint Chapelle - Les Invalides - Versailles

GEOGRAPHY OF PARIS: Capital of France and the region it occupies, the Ile de France, Paris has a population of almost twelve million, the largest in Europe, and an area of 14,518 square kilometers in urban area. It is located in the north of France, specifically north of the great curve of the Seine River. Curiously it is a completely flat city, being perhaps the hill of Montmartre the highest part, with about 130 meters.

LANGUAGE: The original language is French, although you may not find many problems speaking in English or Spanish.
